# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, recruiters, engineering leaders, and product-minded engineers evaluating Karina Macancela for full-stack and frontend-leaning software engineering roles.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio should make Karina's experience easy to understand, credible, and interview-worthy. Success means visitors quickly see seniority, product judgment, technical range, current projects, and clear ways to view LinkedIn, GitHub, resume, or contact Karina.

## Brand Personality

Precise, warm, practical, and product-minded. The site should feel like a polished engineering profile with taste, not a decorative personal blog or generic developer template.

## Anti-references

Avoid random imagery, oversized poster typography, fake terminal/browser chrome, heavy dark-grid aesthetics, repeated numbered section scaffolding, and portfolio pages that bury proof behind vague claims.

## Design Principles

- Lead with role fit and proof before ornament.
- Make project evidence scannable for busy hiring readers.
- Let UI craft signal frontend taste without distracting from the work.
- Keep personal details warm and specific, but secondary to the hiring story.
- Preserve accessible contrast, readable line lengths, and responsive layouts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-friendly navigation, reduced-motion support, and mobile layouts without horizontal overflow or clipped text.
